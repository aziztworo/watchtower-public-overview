# WatchTower

WatchTower is a multilingual AI platform built to enable people to report community issues and rights violations in their own languages, making civic participation accessible to all

## Overview

The platform is designed to make civic reporting accessible, structured, and actionable. It enables individuals and partner organisations to submit incidents through text, voice, and multimedia, and transforms these inputs into structured data that can be analysed in real time. WatchTower connects reporting, analysis, and visibility to strengthen participation and response systems.

## What it does

- Enables public and partner-based reporting of civic incidents in local languages
- Supports text, voice, and multimedia submissions
- Structures and classifies incoming reports for analysis
- Powers real-time pattern detection and trend analysis
- Maps incidents geographically to support situational awareness
- Improves visibility for accountability, response, and decision making
